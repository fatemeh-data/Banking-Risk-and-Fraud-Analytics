# 🏦 Real-World Banking Risk & Fraud Analytics  
**End-to-End Data Analytics Project | SQL • Power BI • Data Modeling**

---

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive end-to-end analytics solution designed to address key challenges in the banking sector, including **credit risk assessment**, **fraud detection**, and **customer behavior analysis**.

Using structured, real-world data, the project transforms raw financial and transactional data into **actionable insights** that support risk mitigation, fraud prevention, and strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Context
Financial institutions must continuously balance growth with risk control. Key challenges include:

- Identifying customers with high default risk  
- Detecting fraudulent transactions in large-scale data  
- Understanding behavioral patterns across diverse customer segments  

This project provides a data-driven framework to tackle these challenges effectively.

## 📊 Dashboard & Analysis

### 🔹 Executive Summary
Provides a consolidated view of performance, risk exposure, and operational metrics.

**Key KPIs:**
- 30,000 Loan Applications  
- $292M+ Total Loan Value  
- 233K+ Transactions  
- 54% Approval Rate  

**Risk Indicators:**
- 7.5% Default Rate  
- 0.58% Fraud Rate  
- 23% High-Risk Customers  

**Business Insight:**
- Balanced approval strategy, but with visible exposure to high-risk segments  
- Fraud presence is relatively low but financially impactful  

---

### 🔹 Credit Risk Analysis
Focuses on understanding risk distribution and default drivers across customer segments.

**Key Metrics:**
- 29,279 Loans Assessed  
- 2,331 High-Risk Customers  
- 44.87% High-Risk Loans  
- $21M+ Defaulted Loan Value  

**Advanced Analysis:**
- Risk segmentation by **income level, job type, and customer category**  
- Credit score classification into **Subprime, Near-Prime, and Prime**  
- Relationship between **risk score and loan approval rates**  

**Key Insights:**
- Risk is significantly higher in lower income segments  
- Credit score is a strong predictor of default probability  
- Loan approvals decrease as risk increases, indicating controlled lending strategy  
- High-risk segments contribute disproportionately to default losses  

**Business Impact:**
- Enables targeted risk control strategies  
- Supports more precise credit approval decisions  
- Reduces exposure to high-default portfolios  

---

### 🔹 Fraud Detection
Identifies patterns and anomalies in transactional behavior to detect fraud.

**Key Metrics:**
- 1,354 Fraudulent Transactions  
- 2,400 Fraudulent Loan Applications  
- $22M+ Fraud Exposure  

**Analysis Highlights:**
- Fraud concentration across specific regions and transaction types  
- Detection based on behavioral signals (e.g., anomalies, inconsistencies)  
- Variation of fraud rates across customer segments  

**Key Insights:**
- Fraud is not random and shows identifiable patterns  
- Certain loan purposes and customer profiles carry higher fraud risk  

**Business Impact:**
- Improves early fraud detection  
- Supports proactive monitoring strategies  
- Minimizes financial loss  

---

### 🔹 Customer Behavior Analysis
Provides insights into demographic and financial behavior patterns.

**Key Metrics:**
- 10,000 Customers  
- 6,970 Active Customers  
- Average Credit Score: 655  
- 8% Default Rate  

**Analysis Highlights:**
- Segmentation by age, income, and job category  
- Behavioral differences across customer types  
- Credit score distribution analysis  

**Key Insights:**
- Customer risk varies significantly across demographic groups  
- Income and job type strongly influence financial behavior  
- Mid-range credit score segment dominates customer base
